Biography
Mingrui Zhang (张铭睿) is a final year Ph.D. student at Imperial College London advised by Prof. Matthew Piggott. He obtained my Master's degree from Imperial College London and Bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University. Previously, he worked with Dr. Tiantian Liu and Dr. Yuanming Hu at Taichi Graphics. His research interests includes physics-based simulation, machine learning and their applications in scientific computing, computer graphics, generative AI.
