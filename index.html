Mingrui Zhang (张铭睿)

About Me

My name is Mingrui Zhang. I am a PhD student at Imperial College London, advised by Matthew Piggott. I obtained my Master degree from Imperial College London and Bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University.

My research focuses on differentiable physical simulation and applying machine learning techniques to enhance simulation.
